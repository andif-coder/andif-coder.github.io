NC229748 - Sum of gcd of Tuples (Hard)

题意

给你nnn, kkk，让你求

∑a1=1k∑a2=1k⋯∑an=1kgcd(a1,a2,⋯ ,an)mod  109+7\sum_{a_1 = 1}^{k}\sum_{a_2 = 1}^{k} \cdots \sum_{a_n = 1}^{k} gcd(a_1, a_2, \cdots, a_n) \mod 10^9 + 7∑a1​=1k​∑a2​=1k​⋯∑an​=1k​gcd(a1​,a2​,⋯,an​)mod109+7
